Emmanuel Sillis
            Emmanuel Sillis is a serial entrepreneur, visionary, and the driving force behind NextGen AI Technology. With over a decade of hands-on experience at the crossroads of AI, automation, management strategy development, and real-world financial management, he has been immersed in complex business operations and technology from an early age.
            His leadership has yielded unprecedented results. At the helm of ACE Media Management, Emmanuel managed a dynamic ecosystem of over 5 million content creators. He spearheaded the development of 'Falcon', a proprietary AI developed back in 2019. This technology was the first of its kind, outperforming major financial institutions and top analysts through advanced, AI-driven social engineering. This groundbreaking ecosystem, integrating LVGroup, ACE Technologies, and IT-Direct, proved his ability to build game-changing solutions at scale.
            His 'power to the people' ethos extends beyond business. As a philanthropist, he challenged traditional structures through his personal initiative, 'The Oracle Of Afrikerke'. Through the renowned 'PayPal Hours' program, he directly invested €70,000 annually into his social media followers, providing them with the tools and resources to achieve financial independence. This initiative empowered countless individuals to escape debt, serving as a direct testament to his 'us vs. institutions' philosophy.
            As CEO of AMS Sillis, and carrying this legacy of innovation forward, Emmanuel now focuses on one mission: to build empathetic tech ecosystems that make powerful tools accessible to everyone. His work combines strategic cost optimization and AI-driven solutions with a focus on transparency, ethical impact, and fostering genuine human connections.
            "Technology should not intimidate—it should liberate. With AI, we can create systems that work for people, not against them. That is the future I am building with NextGen.”
